ResourcePack_ElementalDimensions/assets/elementaldimensions/textures/entity/[entity_name].png

TEXTURE CREATION TIPS:
----------------------
1. Use Minecraft-style pixel art (16x16 pixels)
2. Keep colors consistent with dimension theme:
   - Dreaming Depths: Purples, soft blues, ethereal whites
   - Celestine Expanse: Bright yellows, golds, sky blues
   - Fungal Dominion: Greens, browns, bioluminescent blues
   - Forgotten Archive: Dusty browns, parchment yellows, ink blacks
   - Astral Frontier: Deep purples, star whites, cosmic blues
   - Gloomy Caverns: Dark grays, blacks, dim purples

3. Tools you can use:
   - Piskel (free online): https://www.piskelapp.com/
   - GIMP (free download): https://www.gimp.org/
   - Paint.NET (free, Windows): https://www.getpaint.net/
   - Aseprite (paid but excellent): https://www.aseprite.org/

4. You can also find texture packs online for inspiration, but DO NOT copy textures directly.
